A letter to you

written by Jay, for Lilly
My dear Lilian,

I never planned for this to become an apology — I started building this for us, because you deserve something beautiful. But here we are, and maybe this is exactly what it was always meant to be.

I made you go through things you never should have. I made your life harder because of my own wrong decisions, and I can't pretend otherwise. I'm not going to dress it up. What I did was wrong, and you didn't deserve any of it.

What I can tell you is what happened after. The moment I realized what I was risking, I let go of everything that wasn't you. I cut off what needed to be cut. I chose loyalty — not because I had to, but because there was never actually a choice when it came to you.

Since that day, I haven't had eyes for anyone else. I am attached to you in a way I can't explain and don't want to. You're in everything I do. You're why I built this — every photo, every video, every memory — because losing you made me realize how full life was when you were in it.

I know sorry isn't enough on its own. I know words are easy. But I'm asking you to look at us — look at these memories — and consider that what we have is rare. If you give me another chance, I will spend every day making sure you never question my loyalty again. I promise you won't regret it.

I love you, Lilian. I always will. And I'll wait.

your Jay — always yours, even now
